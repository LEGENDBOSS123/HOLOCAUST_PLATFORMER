Hello. In this game, you are Jojo Betzler, from Jojo Rabbit, and will rescue 4 Jews. They will tell you about their lives inside the labor camps. They will also help you defeat the other prisons in order for you to rescue the rest of the prisons.

Keybinds:
Up or W or Spacebar to jump
A or left arrow to move left
D or right arrow to move right
1 to activate the 1st/topmost ability
2 to activate the 2nd/middle ability
3 to activate 3rd/bottommost ability.

These levels are meant to be hard. Press the skip button to skip
